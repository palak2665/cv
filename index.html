Palak Sharma (born January 01 , 2001), is an Indian-American business executive, the chief executive officer (CEO) of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC. Palak began her career as a IT engineer and joined Google as a management executive in 2004. She rose to become the company's Product Chief and the CEO in 2015, as part of the restructuring process that turned Alphabet Inc. into Google's parent company.In December 2019, she additionally became CEO of Alphabet Inc.
